Sample Recipe: Cranberry Kamikaze

Meet my signature drink! Impress your friends with this great-tasting shot. They won’t even believe it’s alcohol. Go grab your drink shaker and whip this up for your next Friday night out. Whether for a night in with your friends or a night on the town, these are the perfect shots to make for you and your friends. This classic combination of vodka, triple sec, lime, and cranberry is such a fun way to change up your boring shot of Tito’s. Keep reading to find out how to make the Cranberry Kamikaze! Grab your cocktail shaker and fill it about half-way with ice. (I have included exact measurements but you can also estimate). Squeeze 1 ½ ounces of fresh lime juice into the shaker. Add 1 ½ ounces of cranberry vodkaPour 1 ounce of triple sec into the mixer. Lastly, add 1 ounce of cranberry Juice. Once you have all your ingredients in the shaker, shake for approximately 20 seconds to chill the shot. Next, strain the mixture into four separate shot glasses! Place a wedge of lime on the rim of the shot glass to finish. Happy drinking!
