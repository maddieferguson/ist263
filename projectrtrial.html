CORONA SUNRISE

This drink has no relation to Covid-19 and only to Corona Extra beer. This is one of my go-to drinks that is always a crowd-pleaser. If you have tequila, orange juice, a bottle of Corona, and some limes you can make this drink! Perfect for a day on the beach or your night-time happy hour, anytime is a good time for a Corona.

How to Make a Corona Sunrise

  1. First, grab your Corona and take a nice long sip. Drink the beer until the level is even with the top of the label on the bottle.
  2. Next, grab your tequila and fill it up to about where the bottleâ€™s neck begins to narrow. Pour orange juice to the top of the bottle, leaving a little room for a splash of grenadine.
  3. Add a splash of grenadine to make the Corona bottle completely full again.
  4. Garnish with a wedge of lime and enjoy! This super easy drink requires very few ingredients and always hits the spot. Happy drinking!
